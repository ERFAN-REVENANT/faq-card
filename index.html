FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Frontend Mentor has both free and paid plans. The free plan includes a selection of challenges, while the premium plan gives you access to more advanced challenges and additional resources.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Yes, you can use Frontend Mentor projects in your portfolio. These projects are designed to simulate real-world scenarios and help you build a strong portfolio.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

You can get help from the Frontend Mentor community, join their Slack channel, or view solutions submitted by other users for inspiration.
